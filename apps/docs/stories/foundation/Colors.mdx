## Usage

### Brand Colors
Brand colors are used to represent our brand identity and create visual consistency across the application.

- **Primary-500**: Main brand color, used for primary actions and key UI elements
- **Primary-400**: Used for hover states and secondary actions
- **Primary-600**: Used for active states and emphasis
- **Primary-700**: Used for text on light backgrounds
- **Primary-800**: Used for headings and important text

### Semantic Colors
Semantic colors are used to communicate specific states and actions.

- **Success**: Used for positive actions, success states, and confirmations
- **Warning**: Used for cautionary actions and warning states
- **Danger**: Used for destructive actions and error states
